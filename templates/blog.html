Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App: AI-Powered Healthcare by Divyam Vijayvargia

In the modern era of AI and data-driven healthcare, early and accurate diagnosis is crucial. As a Computer Science student at VIT Vellore, I have always been passionate about integrating technology with real-world problems. With my expertise in machine learning and web development, I developed a **Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App** using **Flask** and a **Decision Tree Classifier model** to assist users in identifying potential health conditions based on their symptoms.

The Problem

Healthcare accessibility remains a major challenge, and many individuals struggle to differentiate between similar symptoms of various diseases. With growing reliance on technology, there is a need for **an intelligent, AI-powered system that can provide quick health insights**. This project addresses that gap by enabling users to input symptoms and receive accurate predictions regarding potential illnesses.

The Solution

With my background in **machine learning, data science, and full-stack development**, I designed a system that allows users to enter their symptoms, which are then processed by a **pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model**. Hereâ€™s how it works:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms through a **clean and intuitive web interface**, making healthcare insights accessible to everyone.
  2. Machine Learning Model: The Decision Tree Classifier model, trained on a comprehensive dataset of symptoms and diseases, predicts the **most probable illness**.
  3. Prediction & Insights: The app not only predicts potential diseases but also provides **detailed information on symptoms, causes, precautions, and treatment recommendations**.
  4. Personalized Health Guidance: Users receive **dietary recommendations, workout tips, and essential nutrient suggestions** (e.g., iron, calcium) to improve overall health.

Key Features

  • Developed by an AI/ML Enthusiast: Built by Divyam Vijayvargia, leveraging **machine learning, data science, and healthcare analytics**.
  • Accurate AI Predictions: The model ensures precise and reliable **disease predictions** using an extensive dataset.
  • Comprehensive Health Information: In addition to diagnosis, users get **medical insights, precautions, and lifestyle tips**.
  • Education & Awareness: The platform offers **blogs and articles** on various health topics to help users stay informed.

Conclusion

This project is an example of how **artificial intelligence and healthcare can merge to create impactful solutions**. With my experience in **AI, ML, and full-stack web development**, I aim to make healthcare **more accessible, data-driven, and personalized**. This is just the beginning of my journey toward **innovative AI-powered healthcare solutions**.
